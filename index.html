“How can we leverage graph analytics, generative AI and other data approaches to optimise public school transport networks?”

Meet SchoolRoute, a web application designed to provide students and parents with essential information needed to help them decide which route is the best to take.

SchoolRoute is a student-first, data-driven web app that improves school transport by providing real-time and historical insights on bus route reliability, accessibility, and usage. We use an AI chatbot to deliver clear, student-friendly updates to support safer, more predictable, and efficient travel decisions.
